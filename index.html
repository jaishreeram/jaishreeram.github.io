Short Biography

Prof. Rajendra Sahu" is professor at ABV-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior. Dr. Sahu has done his PhD from IIT, Kharagpur, after completing his master's degree in Management and in Engineering.

He has been associated with the teaching profession for the past 30 years, and has been closely involved with industrial consultancy projects, and as an external Consultant to various organizations like IIT, Kharagpur, Educational Consultant of India etc. He was the Secretary of Systems Dynamics Society of India.

IT and Management Consultancy has been his forte. He has been associated with more than 20 prestigious projects in the past, and has guided 08 PhD, 355 MTech / MBA thesis and has published more than 100 papers in his areas of proficiency and interest. He is on the editorial board of 07 international journals.

His primary areas of interest are Systems Approach to Management, Business Analytics, IT-enabled Services, Data Warehousing, and Data Mining.

He was Director, AP-International Institute of Information Technology, Basar for 2 terms. He was the ex-advisor to the Chancellor, RGUKT, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh.

Prof Sahu has been awarded as the Best Professor in Knowledge Management by 26th Business School Affaire & Dewang Mehta National Education Awards. He has been honoured with the prestigious Dr. Abdul Kalam Gold Medal, 2015, as a Lifetime Achievement Award to distinguished and meritorious Indian personalities for his outstanding individual achievements in the field of education and research.
